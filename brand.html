NEW HUNDREDS

Brand Guide


My brother made a clothing and I decided to use it to make a website. It's called New Hundreds. It can be described as an urban brand, specializing in hoodies and sweats. We tried doing it on build-your-own platform, but I felt the options were limited. I felt like this would be a great project to do this semester.

"Your New Standard"

Brand Theme

This was a no brainer. I used the colors of a dollar bill for the theme. A great collection of muted colors that stand out great on the grayish background. For the fonts I chose an art deco pairing that gives it a classic elegance. And I may possibly use a gradient to add some texture to the screen.
